Reflected XSS

What it is

Payload travels in the HTTP request and is immediately echoed in the response. No persistence, but one-click pwn.

Why it happens

Template engines print request parameters verbatim: <input value="<?=$_GET['q']?>">. Break out of quotes → code.

Where you’ll meet it

Search bars, 404 pages, tracking parameters, preview modes, payment flows (“returnUrl”).

Impact

One-shot credential steal, payment hijack, OAuth token redirect, cache poison → becomes stored for others.

Mini case-study

2022 Twitter “return_to” reflected XSS → stole admin session on ads.twitter.com → $7 000 bounty.
